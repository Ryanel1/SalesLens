# Product

## Register

product

## Users

SalesLens is used by Ryan Lester/Lester Sales to review retail account performance, upload POS and inventory reports, generate shareable account reports, and inspect product-level sales and inventory behavior. The main use context is focused business review work where numbers must feel trustworthy and easy to explain to a buyer or account partner.

## Product Purpose

SalesLens turns account spreadsheets into a polished sales and inventory report for Volshop and Rebel Rags. Success means the app imports source reports accurately, preserves account-specific rules, shows current performance clearly, and makes shared report links match the authenticated dashboard presentation.

## Brand Personality

Bold, sporty, direct. The product should feel confident and retail-performance driven without making the working controls loud or confusing.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS dashboards, mismatched control styles, decorative complexity, ambiguous import flows, and any UI copy that makes sales and inventory uploads feel like the same kind of data when they are not.

## Design Principles

- Trust the numbers first: every import and metric should communicate what source data it represents.
- Separate workflows when the underlying data behaves differently.
- Keep report presentation bold, but make operational controls quiet and exact.
- Match authenticated and shared report surfaces wherever customers see the same information.
- Prefer explicit review states over hidden assumptions during upload/import.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, visible focus states, clear labels for upload/import actions, keyboard-accessible modal controls, and reduced ambiguity for loading, empty, zero, and replaced-data states.
